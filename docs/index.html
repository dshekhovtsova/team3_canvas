Why n8n: Our Choice for Orchestration

We selected n8n as the core orchestration engine for our POC due to its compelling advantages that directly address the needs of an efficient and scalable AI workflow.

💲

Cost-Effectiveness

Its predictable pricing model ensures scalability without escalating costs, making it ideal for high-volume operations and budget control.

🔒

Data Control & Sovereignty

The self-hosting capability allows us to keep sensitive lead data in-house, which is crucial for compliance and data privacy regulations.

🧩

Flexibility & Customization

As a developer-friendly platform, n8n allows for custom code and bespoke integrations, adapting seamlessly to evolving needs and unique workflows.

📈

Scalability & Growth

It offers predictable costs and granular control over infrastructure, enabling seamless scaling as our lead generation efforts expand and mature.

Key Terms Explained:

  • Workflow Execution (n8n): In n8n, an "execution" refers to a single complete run of an entire workflow, regardless of how many steps or operations are contained within that workflow. This offers predictable pricing as you pay per workflow run, not per individual action.
  • Request / Operation (APIs): For APIs like Web Scraper or Spam Checker, a "request" or "operation" typically means a single call made to that API. Each time your system asks the API to perform a specific task (e.g., scrape a page, check for spam), it counts as one request.

Success Criteria for Our POC

Our Proof of Concept will be deemed successful based on achieving the following measurable outcomes within one month of operation.

💰

High-Value Lead Generation

Acquire at least 1 to 2 new leads, each with the potential to generate $50,000 in revenue within one month of the POC.

📈

Improved Reply Rates

Demonstrate a significant increase in email reply rates, targeting 6-7% (considered a very good result for outsourcing) or at least 3-4% (which is considered not bad, compared to current 1-2%).
